"Yoga is a journey of the self, through the self, to the self." -The Bhagavad Gita

My journey with yoga began several years ago. After attending my first class, I knew that yoga would be in my life forever. Finally, I had found something that clicked with me immediately. It was as if I had found something I had been unknowingly searching for my entire life. After several years of practice, I decided that I wanted to share yoga with others. This desire led me to India where I studied Hatha and Ashtanga yoga in the beautiful Himalayas.

What I love about yoga is that it always meets you where you are. You don't have to be a certain size or age. You don't have to have a certain amount of strength or knowledge. Whoever you are, wherever you are- yoga is right there.
